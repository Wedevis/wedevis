2048 Boom is a new way to play 2048!
Play the Boom Mode: avoid the explosions and keep your tiles in game.
You can also play the "Classic Mode" for a standard 2048 experience.

Personalize your game with skins and reach new record in the global leaderboards!
Privacy Policy The game asks you to login with your Google Play Games account in order to record your highscores in the leadearboards, view the achievements and may save the game data in the cloud.
Wedevis can't access to your personal informations, everything is processed by Google and its services.
Google shares with us different stats of our players, in anonymous and aggregate manner, meaning you could not be personally identified from it.
